Cybercrime Quiz 2

1. Which of the following is a type of cyberattack that floods a network or server with excessive traffic, rendering it unavailable to users?

  • Phishing
  • DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)
  • Spoofing
Correct Answer: DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)

2. What term describes the unauthorized access, use, or manipulation of electronic data stored on a computer system?

  • Phishing
  • Cyberextortion
  • Hacking
Correct Answer: Hacking

3. What is the term used to describe a fraudulent scheme that relies on the manipulation of individuals to obtain sensitive information or access to systems?

  • Ransomware
  • Social Engineering
  • Malware
Correct Answer: Social Engineering

4. Which of the following is a malicious software program that encrypts files and demands payment for their release?

  • Spyware
  • Trojan
  • Ransomware
Correct Answer: Ransomware

5. What term refers to the practice of deceiving individuals into providing sensitive information by pretending to be a trustworthy entity?

  • Phishing
  • Spoofing
  • Cyberbullying
Correct Answer: Phishing

6. What term describes the act of accessing someone else's computer system without authorization to obtain information or disrupt normal operations?

  • Cyberbullying
  • Cyberstalking
  • Unauthorized Access
Correct Answer: Unauthorized Access

7. What is the term used to describe the practice of altering, destroying, or stealing electronic information?

  • Cyberterrorism
  • Cybercrime
  • Cyberbullying
Correct Answer: Cybercrime

8. What term refers to the use of email or other electronic communications to harass, intimidate, or threaten an individual or group?

  • Cyberterrorism
  • Cyberstalking
  • Cyberbullying
Correct Answer: Cyberstalking

9. What term refers to the use of computer systems or networks to disrupt the functioning of a society for political or ideological reasons?

  • Cyberterrorism
  • Cyberextortion
  • Cyberbullying
Correct Answer: Cyberterrorism

10. What term refers to the act of illegally copying and distributing software, movies, music, or other digital content?

  • Cyberespionage
  • Cyberextortion
  • Piracy
Correct Answer: Piracy
