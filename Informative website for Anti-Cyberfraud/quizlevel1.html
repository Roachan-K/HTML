Cybercrime Quiz 1

1. What is the term used to describe the act of illegally accessing a computer system or network?

  • Hacking
  • Phishing
  • Spoofing
Correct Answer: Hacking

2. What term refers to the fraudulent practice of sending emails purporting to be from reputable companies to trick individuals into revealing personal information?

  • Hacking
  • Phishing
  • Malware
Correct Answer: Phishing

3. What is the term used to describe the practice of creating a fake website that resembles a legitimate one in order to deceive visitors and obtain their personal information?

  • Hacking
  • Spoofing
  • Pharming
Correct Answer: Pharming

4. What term describes a type of malware that restricts access to a computer system until a ransom is paid?

  • Ransomware
  • Spyware
  • Trojan
Correct Answer: Ransomware

5. What is the term used to describe the unauthorized copying, distribution, or use of copyrighted materials?

  • Piracy
  • Counterfeiting
  • Identity Theft
Correct Answer: Piracy

6. What term refers to the act of using someone else's identity, typically for financial gain?

  • Phishing
  • Identity Theft
  • Hacking
Correct Answer: Identity Theft

7. What is the term used to describe the practice of sending unsolicited bulk messages, typically advertising, to a large number of recipients?

  • Hacking
  • Spamming
  • Spoofing
Correct Answer: Spamming

8. What term refers to the act of gaining unauthorized access to information or data stored on a computer system?

  • Phishing
  • Hacking
  • Spoofing
Correct Answer: Hacking

9. What is the term used to describe the act of creating and distributing malicious software with the intent to cause harm?

  • Phishing
  • Spoofing
  • Malware
Correct Answer: Malware

10. What is the term used to describe the act of stealing someone's personal information and using it without permission, typically for financial gain?

  • Identity Theft
  • Phishing
  • Hacking
Correct Answer: Identity Theft
